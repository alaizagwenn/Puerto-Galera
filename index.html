Beaches & Relaxation

Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and unwinding by the sea.

White Beach

The most famous spot; lively with restaurants, water sports, and beautiful sunsets.

Talipanan Beach

Peaceful and scenic; ideal for quiet relaxation and long walks.

Aninuan Beach

Calm waters, perfect for swimming and sunset viewing.

Bayanan Beach

A serene stop often included in island-hopping tours.

Small La Laguna Beach

A cozy cove with calm waters, perfect for families and snorkeling.

Sabang Beach

Known for its vibrant diving scene and beautiful coastal views.

Water Activities & Marine Adventures

For thrill-seekers and ocean lovers.

Snorkeling & Diving
  • Sabang Beach Dive Sites - World-class diving locations
  • Coral Garden - Excellent snorkeling with colorful corals
  • Sinandigan Reef - Biodiversity hotspot for advanced divers
Island-Hopping Highlights
  • Underwater Cave (San Antonio Island)
  • Sandbar (near Boquete Island)
  • Boquete Island & Coral Garden
Water Sports

Available mostly at White Beach:

  • Jet ski
  • Banana boat ride
  • Parasailing
  • Flyboarding

Nature & Land Attractions

Discover waterfalls, culture, and panoramic viewpoints.

Tamaraw Falls

Beautiful twin waterfalls with a natural pool at the base.

Mangyan Village (Talipanan)

Home of the Iraya Mangyan tribe; known for handmade crafts.

Sinandigan Lighthouse

Offers panoramic ocean views; great for photos.

Mount Malasimbo

Popular hiking area and venue for the annual arts and music festival.

Puerto Galera Bay

A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve with stunning coastal views.

Muelle Bay

The main harbor with colorful boats and waterfront dining options.

Leisure & Cultural Experiences

Enjoy food, views, and cultural immersion.

The View Point

Offers a stunning view of Puerto Galera Bay, perfect for sunset dining or cocktails.

Great mix of local and Western dishes
Ideal for photo ops during golden hour
Floating Bars (Sabang)

Relax on boats! Enjoy music and drinks while floating on the sea during the day.

Fire Dancing Shows (White Beach)

Evening performances that light up the beach with cultural entertainment.

Travel Tips

How to Get There:

From Manila → Bus to Batangas Pier → Ferry to Muelle Pier, Puerto Galera.

Best Time to Visit:

November – May (dry season).

Where to Stay:
  • For lively vibes: White Beach or Sabang
  • For peace & relaxation: Talipanan or Aninuan
What to Bring:
  • Sunscreen
  • Snorkel gear
  • Insect repellent
  • Waterproof bag
  • Cash (ATMs are limited)
UNESCO Recognition

Puerto Galera is recognized as a UNESCO Man and Biosphere Reserve, protecting its rich biodiversity including coral reefs, mangrove forests, and diverse marine life.
